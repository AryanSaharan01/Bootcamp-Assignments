K.R. Mangalam University

K.R. Mangalam University is a private university established in 2013 and located in Sohna Road, Gurugram, Haryana, India. It is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC).

History

The university was established by the K.R. Mangalam Group through the Haryana Private Universities Act. It aims to deliver world-class education and skill-based training in various disciplines.

Campus

The campus is situated in a scenic area on Sohna Road with modern infrastructure, labs, libraries, auditoriums, and hostels. It promotes innovation and holistic development.

Academics

  • Engineering and Technology
  • Management and Commerce
  • Law
  • Pharmacy
  • Basic & Applied Sciences

Affiliations

The university is affiliated with UGC and other regulatory bodies like BCI (for Law), PCI (for Pharmacy), etc.
